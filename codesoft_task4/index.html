Allama Muhammad Iqbal

Allama Muhammad Iqbal, revered as the "Poet of the East" and the "Spiritual Father of Pakistan," stands as an iconic figure whose legacy transcends borders and generations. Born in Sialkot, Pakistan, in 1877, Iqbal emerged as a visionary philosopher, poet, and thinker whose profound insights continue to resonate with millions worldwide. His life journey, marked by a relentless pursuit of knowledge, a deep spiritual awakening, and an unwavering commitment to the upliftment of humanity, serves as an enduring source of inspiration.
